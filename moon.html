Moon Landing Hoax

Moon landing conspiracy theories claim that some or all elements of the Apollo program and the associated Moon landings were hoaxes staged by NASA. This conspiracy theory is supported by arguments about the ability of the flag to move, the pressure placed on the US government to beat Russia in the space race.

Motivation for the United States to engage the Soviet Union in a Space Race can be traced to the then on-going Cold War. Landing on the Moon was viewed as a national and technological accomplishment that would generate world-wide acclaim.

One major clue conspiracists cite is the flapping US flag planted by Apollo 11’s crew arguing that the lack of atmosphere in space should have stopped its ability to wave. Another theory is that the shadows in the Moon landing photos prove the images were faked and conspiracists argue that if the Sun were the only light source, then the shadows should be parallel? Doesn't this prove that the whole scene was mocked up in a studio, with multiple light sources creating different shadow patterns?
