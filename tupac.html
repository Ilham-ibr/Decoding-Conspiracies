Tupac Shakur's Death

Tupac Shakur was murdered in a drive-by shooting in 1996 but many believe he didn’t succumb to gunshot wounds sustained in a drive-by shooting on the Las Vegas Strip on 13 September 1996, but escaped to Cuba, and has been laughing at us ever since.

The identity of the chart-topper’s killer has remained a mystery for more than 20 years and no charges have ever been brought. There have been countless 'sightings' over the years, as well as conspiracy theories about the rapper and his location.

But perhaps the most baffling theory sees people claim Tupac actually hinted that he's still alive in his 2002 posthumous release track Better Dayz. "Expect me like you expect Jesus to come back," the song went. Before he died, he started calling himself Makaveli, a reference to the Italian philosopher Niccolò Machiavelli. Some fans believe Makaveli was an anagram meaning "Mak Alive".
