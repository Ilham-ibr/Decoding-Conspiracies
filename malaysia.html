Malaysian Airlines Flight

On the 8th March 2014, Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 was a scheduled international passenger flight operated by Malaysia Airlines. It disappeared while flying from Kuala Lumpur International Airport to its planned destination, Beijing Capital International Airport. Speculation has mounted about the possibility that hijackers took the plane to a remote island, although no group has claimed responsibility.

Unofficial researchers have identified more than 600 possible runways at which the plane was capable of landing. A mathematics professor from Texas has argued that the plane must have entered the sea vertically; any other angle of entry would have splintered the airplane to many pieces. Many argue this supports the claim the plane was hijacked and driven into the oceans.
