Pharmaceutical Industry

The Big Pharma conspiracy theory is a group of conspiracy theories that claim that the medical community in general and pharmaceutical companies in particular, especially large corporations, operate for sinister purposes and against the public good, and that they allegedly cause and worsen a wide range of diseases.

Kevin Treadeu argued in his book about natural cures that there are all-natural cures for serious illnesses such as cancer, herpes and obesity that are being hidden from the public by the FDA and other food and drug companies.

This conspiracy is especially popular by those who believe the cure for cancer exists and has been hidden for years now. They believe that cancer is an illness and that profits big business heavily and by providing the cure, this would limit profit.
