Nelson Mandela's Death

South Africa social media argued that Nelson Mandela actually died in 1985. Mandela was known in South Africa as the man who brought liberation into a country plagued by apartheid however to some he was known as a sell out.

They believe that the actual Mandela died in 1985 at the age of 67 and that this is why south africans are ecncouraged to do 67 minutes of charity every year. The reason being he was killed to be replaced by a more neutral imposter after being released from prison who negotiated peace instead of his previously radical tactics.

An image of a younger Mandela’s face was put into the popular FaceApp application. FaceApp “revealed” that its version of an aged Mandela looked nothing like the man released to the world from Victor Verster Prison on February 11, 1990.
